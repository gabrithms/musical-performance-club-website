Welcome!

Welcome to the Musical Performance Club at Abbey Park! Whether you sing, play an instrument, or just love music, this is the club to create and perform! This club will give you the chance to learn new instruments, play with friends, and take parts in concerts and events throughout the year. No matter your level, everyone is welcome!

JOIN US IN ROOM 109 EVERY MONDAY!

What do we do at meetings?

  • Lessons about music, songwriting, genres, and history 🎶
  • Themed concerts like duet night, 80s night, and rock night 🎸
  • Practice and learn new instruments in workshops 🎹
  • Prepare for big and exciting events at Abbey Park! 👩‍🎤
